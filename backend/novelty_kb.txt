**NOVEL Contributions of the Research Paper "Attention Is All You Need":**

- **New Idea:** Introduction of the Transformer architecture, which relies solely on attention mechanisms, eliminating the need for recurrent and convolutional networks in sequence transduction tasks.

- **Methodology:** The Transformer uses a self-attention mechanism to process input sequences in parallel, enhancing efficiency and reducing training time.

- **Improvement Over Prior Work:** Achieves superior performance on machine translation tasks, with a notable improvement of over 2 BLEU points on English-to-German translation compared to the best existing models, and establishes a new state-of-the-art BLEU score for English-to-French translation.

- **Unique Contribution:** Demonstrates the versatility of the Transformer by successfully applying it to other tasks, such as English constituency parsing, showcasing its generalization capabilities across different types of data.
